PickDatePlace

1.0.0 · Prepare for Submission

Choose the date first, then the place.

PickDatePlace (PDP) makes group planning simple: decide when first, then everything else falls into place. Create a poll in iMessage, share time options, and let everyone vote Yes / Maybe / No in seconds. PDP highlights the best overlap automatically so your group can lock a date fast, without endless chat threads.

Distribution: TestFlight / Xcode Cloud

Store Status: iOS App 1.0.0 in review prep

Current Build: 11 (version 1.0.0)

Planned Release: no earlier than Feb 9, 2026 2:00 PM GMT

Languages: English (U.S.)

In-App Purchase: Monthly Organizer subscription

  • iOS
  • iMessage App
  • Group Scheduling
  • Availability Polls
  • Apple Calendar Integration

Why PDP

  • Create date/time polls in just a few taps.
  • Share directly in iMessage conversations.
  • Collect availability from your group quickly.
  • See the best matching time at a glance.
  • Lock the winning option and finalize plans.

Great For

  • Dinner plans
  • Team meetups
  • Family events
  • Study groups
  • Weekend activities

PawMode

1.0.0 · In Development

Monitor your vehicle's temperature and keep your pets safe.

PawMode turns a spare iPhone into a Tesla-style Dog Mode for any vehicle. Leave it in your car or RV to monitor temperature, display a live camera feed with surveillance indicators, and show reassurance messages to passersby. Pair a second device to get remote alerts, snapshots, and full control from your pocket.

Distribution: Direct build / internal testing

Store Status: Not yet submitted

Current Build: In development

Planned Release: TBD

Languages: English (U.S.) + auto-translation

In-App Purchase: Premium subscription (weekly / monthly / yearly / lifetime)

  • iOS
  • iPad
  • Thermal Monitoring
  • Live Camera Feed
  • Two-Device Pairing
  • Remote Alerts
  • CloudKit Sync

Why PawMode

  • Real-time thermal state monitoring with color-coded alerts.
  • Live front-camera feed for visual deterrence.
  • Two-device pairing for remote monitoring from your pocket.
  • Customizable messages that auto-translate to the local language.
  • Push notifications when temperature reaches critical levels.

Great For

  • Pet owners with dogs or cats in the car
  • RV and camper van travelers
  • Anti-theft deterrence while parked
  • Reassuring passersby that your pets are safe
  • Solo travelers and car campers
