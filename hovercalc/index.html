macOS Menu Bar Utility

HoverCalc

A simple, powerful calculator that lives in your menu bar. Detach it into a floating window for desktop and fullscreen apps.

Features

Calculate anywhere, anytime

HoverCalc stays out of the way until you need it. Click the menu bar icon or press a hotkey to start calculating instantly.

Menu Bar Calculator

Click the menu bar icon to open HoverCalc as a popup panel. Type your expression and get results instantly.

Floating Mode

Drag the title bar to detach HoverCalc into a floating window. It stays on top of all apps, even in fullscreen.

Expression Input

Type full expressions like 245*18 or (100+50)/3. See your expression and result at the same time.

Global Hotkey

Press ⌘ + = to toggle HoverCalc from any app. Fully configurable in Settings.

Calculation History

Recent calculations are saved so you can quickly recall previous results. Tap any entry to reuse it.

Open at Login

Enable launch at login so HoverCalc is always one click or keypress away. Set it once and forget it.
