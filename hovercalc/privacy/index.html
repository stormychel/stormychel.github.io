HoverCalc Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how HoverCalc ("App") handles information when you use the app on Apple platforms.

1. Data We Process

  • HoverCalc primarily performs calculations locally on your device.
  • Recent calculation history may be stored locally for convenience.
  • No account sign-up is required for core app functionality.

2. How Data Is Used

Data is used only to operate core app functionality, including expression input, result display, and local history features.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data and do not share app data with third parties for advertising.

4. Data Retention

Any locally stored calculator data remains on your device until removed by you or by uninstalling the app.

5. Security

We rely on Apple platform protections (including app sandboxing) to help protect data processed by the app.

6. Children's Privacy

HoverCalc is a general-purpose utility app and is not intended to collect personal data from children.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top indicates the latest version.

8. Contact
