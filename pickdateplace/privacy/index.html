PickDatePlace Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PickDatePlace ("App") handles information when you use the app on Apple platforms.

1. Data We Process

  • Poll content you enter (titles, descriptions, date/time options).
  • Participation responses (availability selections).
  • Purchase and entitlement information provided by Apple StoreKit.

2. How Data Is Used

We use data only to operate core app functionality: creating polls, recording responses, syncing app state between app and iMessage extension, and validating paid organizer access.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Payment transactions are handled by Apple, and Apple may process related purchase data under Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

Poll and response data is retained on-device and associated storage used by the app unless removed by the user or by uninstalling the app.

5. Security

We use platform-provided protections (Apple frameworks and app sandboxing). No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

PickDatePlace is rated 4+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal data online from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data in connection with the App, contact us and we will review and remove it where appropriate.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top indicates the latest version.

8. Contact
